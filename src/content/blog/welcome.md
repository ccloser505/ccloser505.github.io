This is a small space for thinking out loud — about research, reading, and the slow work of becoming a sociologist.

I expect most posts will fall into one of three categories: brief notes on papers I'm reading, methodological musings (mostly about quantitative work with Korean panel data), and the occasional reflection on academic life from somewhere between the master's and the doctoral.

Posts here are deliberately rough. They aren't polished publications — more like marginalia made public.

If you'd like to get in touch, you can reach me by [email](mailto:ccloser505@gmail.com).
