Presented at the Korean Sociological Association annual meeting in Seoul. The talk — "The Shifting Relationship Between Marriage, Partnership, and Reproduction" — traced changes in South Korean women's attitudes toward non-marital cohabitation and childbirth from 2010 to 2022, drawing on nationally representative survey data.
