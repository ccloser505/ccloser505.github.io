A new paper, co-authored with Yool Choi, has been accepted at the *Korea Journal of Population Studies*. The paper traces how childcare arrangements among Korean families shifted between 2010 and 2020 using microdata from the Population and Housing Census. We document a pronounced reduction in extended-family care alongside the rise of institutional childcare, with stark differences across socioeconomic strata.
