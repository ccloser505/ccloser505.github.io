I'll be giving an invited talk at the *Workshop on Family Formation in Transition* hosted by the University at Buffalo. The talk — "Between 'Deficit' and 'Choice': Shifting Dynamics of Non-marital Childbearing in South Korea, 1997–2024" — draws on three decades of attitudinal and birth-record data to trace how non-marital childbearing has been re-narrated, both demographically and discursively.

This is joint work with Yool Choi and Jiwon Lee. The abstract has also been selected for the *Journal of Comparative Family Studies* special issue.
