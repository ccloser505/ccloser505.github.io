Invited to speak at the *Public Seminar: Family and Children in East Asia* at Keio University, Tokyo. The talk — "Decoupling Partnership and Reproduction from Marriage: Period Effects on Korean Women's Attitudes toward Non-marital Cohabitation and Childbearing after 2010" — focused on how attitudes toward non-marital family forms have shifted after 2010, with comparative angles toward Japanese and other East Asian trajectories.
